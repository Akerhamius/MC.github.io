Metallic Crusade

Metallic Crusade is a homebrewed version of Kill Team 2nd Edition with additions from Kill Team 1st Edition and some inspiration from Warhammer 40k. More importantly, this homebrewed system was not created for the purpose of being super balanced or optimized but to have fun with it.

The Core Rules for this version are exactly the same as they are for Kill Team 2nd Edition. New additions and modifications include units, equipment, ploys and sub-factions and are explained in this site.

Adeptus Mechanicus

Hunter Clade

Preamble

Within the Legiones Skitarii of the Adeptus Mechanicus, the organizational structure is meticulously designed to optimize operational efficiency and adaptability. At the forefront of this structure lies the Clade, the smallest specialized unit within the hierarchy. Traditionally, a Clade consists of various squads, each fulfilling specific roles on the battlefield. However, the Hunter Clade stands apart as a specialized unit tailored for operations demanding finesse and precision.

A Hunter Clade is further subdivided into Microclades, each comprising pairs of Skitarii and Sicarians teams. These Microclades are deployed instead of the entire Hunter Clade, ensuring optimal utilization of resources while maintaining operational flexibility. While most Microclades boast a mix of unit types to navigate diverse mission parameters, certain operations warrant a more specialized approach where a single type of unit is the most effective option. In such cases, Microclades are designated as Strike, Recon, Covert, or Assault, reflecting the predominant unit type within.

Composition

A Microclade consists of 2 teams selected from:

  • Skitarii Vanguard
  • Skitarii Ranger
  • Sicarian Infiltrator
  • Sicarian Ruststalker

A Microclade must have one leader selected from one of its teams.

Skitarii Team

  • Consists of five Skitarii Ranger or Skitarii Vanguard operatives.
  • Can include 1 Surveyor or 1 Diktat.
  • Can include up to 2 Gunners or 3 if your Microclade has a Sicarian team. Each Gunner must be equipped with a different weapon.
  • May choose an Skitarii Ranger Alpha or Skitarii Vanguard Alpha as Leader respectively.

Sicarian Team

  • Consists of five Sicarian Infiltrators or Sicarian Ruststalkers.
  • May choose a Sicarian Infiltrator Princeps or Sicarian Ruststalker Princeps as Leader respectively.

Specialized Microclades

A Microclade composed of two identical teams include one additional operative and passively benefits from a dedicated protocol.

Strike Microclade
  • Composition: 2 Skitarii Vanguard teams.
  • Precision Protocol: Reroll failed hit rolls of 1 when shooting.

Recon Microclade
  • Composition: 2 Skitarii Ranger teams.
  • Sentinel Protocol: Reroll failed saving rolls of 1 against shooting attacks.

Stealth Microclade
  • Composition: 2 Sicarian Infiltrator teams.
  • Stealth Protocol: Reroll failed saving rolls of 1 when benefitting from Cover.

Assault Microclade
  • Composition: 2 Sicarian Ruststalker teams.
  • Spearhead Protocol: Reroll failed hit rolls of 1 when fighting.

Strategic Ploys

Martial Protocol
[1CP]
Vanguards within 6” of objectives/drop zone: Re-roll one attack die for shooting attacks.

Rangers that haven't moved: Re-roll one attack die for shooting attacks.

Accelerant Agents
[1CP]
Ruststalker operatives: Can perform a free Fight action or two during activation until end of Turning Point.

Neurostatic Interference [1CP]
Infiltrator operative within 6” of enemy: Prevent opponent from re-rolling attack dice during combat or shooting attacks.

Calculated Approach [1CP]
Operatives more than 6” from enemy: Retain failed critical saves as successful normal saves until end of Turning Point.

Tactical Ploys

Pursuers
[1CP]
Recon: Two Ranger operatives perform free Dash.

Infiltrate: Change activation order of two ready Ranger operatives.

Command Override
[1CP]
Activate Doctrina Imperative for one operative for one round.

Concealed Position [1CP]
Set up Infiltrator with Conceal order near Heavy terrain, unchangeable during first Turning Point.

Motive Force Vitality [1CP]
Regain D3 lost wounds when ready operative activated.

Tac Ops

Relentless Pursuit
  • Reveal in Target Reveal step of any Turning Point.

  • Score 1VP if no enemy operatives are more than 9" from friendly operatives by end of Turning Point.

  • Score 1VP for achieving first condition in subsequent Turning Points.

Calculated Eradication
  • Reveal when a Doctrina Imperative first becomes active.

  • Score 1VP at end of Turning Point if active Imperative and total wounds lost by enemies exceed those by friendlies.

  • Score 1VP at end of Turning Point if different Imperative is active and total wounds lost by enemies exceed those by friendlies.

Assassination Order
  • Reveal in Target Reveal step of first Turning Point.

  • Opponent selects one operative.

  • Score 1VP if selected enemy operative is incapacitated before fourth Turning Point.

  • Score 1VP if friendly Hunter Clade operative within 3" of selected enemy operative achieves first condition.

Equipment

    Equipment Rules

  • 10 Equipment Points maximum.
  • Only 1 item per operative.
  • Equipment+ can be selected a maximum of once.

Uplinked Vid-Feed
[1EP]
  • Operative: Ranger or Vanguard
  • Ability: No penalty to BS when firing in Overwatch.

Servo-skull+
[2EP]
  • Operative: Any
  • Ability: Once per Turning Point, perform a mission action for -1 AP during activation.

Mechadendrites+
[2EP]
  • Operative: Any
  • Ability: Once per Turning Point, perform Pick Up action for -1 AP during activation.

Cense Bearer
[2EP]
  • Operative: Any
  • Ability: Ignore modifiers to Movement characteristic when within 3” of the Cense Bearer.

Command Uplink+
[4EP]
  • Operative: diktat
  • Ability: Perform COMMAND UPLINK (2AP) to add 1CP, not within 6” of enemy operatives.

Enriched Rounds
[2EP]
  • Operative: Any
  • Ability: Add +1 Damage to selected radium carbine or master-crafted radium pistol.

Optimised Gait
[1EP]
  • Operative: Ruststalker
  • Ability: Ignore the first 2” distance of movement for climbs, drops, or traverses during activation.

Refractor Field+
[3EP]
  • Operative: Leader
  • Ability: 4+ invulnerable save.

Doctrina Imperatives

    Doctrina Imperatives Rules

  • Select a Doctrina Imperative at the beginning of each Strategy Phase and apply its Optimization effect.
  • If a Doctrina Imperative isn't changed, at the beginning of the next round the player will suffer its deprecation effect.

Protector
Imperative

  • Optimisation: Re-roll one attack dice for shooting attacks.
  • Deprecation: Re-roll one critical hit for combat attacks.

Conqueror
Imperative
  • Optimisation: Re-roll one attack dice for combat attacks.
  • Deprecation: Re-roll one critical hit for shooting attacks.

Bulwark
Imperative
  • Optimisation: Re-roll one defence dice for shooting attacks against Hunter Clade operatives.
  • Deprecation: Hunter Clade operatives cannot perform Dash actions.

Aggressor
Imperative
  • Optimisation: Add 1 to the Movement characteristic of Hunter Clade operatives.
  • Deprecation: Defence dice cannot be automatically retained from Cover against shooting attacks.

Equalised
Imperative
  • No effect on Optimisation or Deprecation.

Forge World Dogma

  • Select a Forge World Dogma when creating your Hunter Clade.

Mars
Glory to the Omnissiah
  • Ignore deprecation effect of a Doctrina Imperative or activate a non-active one once per encounter.
  • Can Re-roll one hit, wound, or save roll per turning point.

Graia
Refusal to Yield
  • Roll a dice when reduced to 0 wounds: on 6, restore to 1 wound remaining (once per operative).
  • Roll a dice when losing a wound from mortal wound: on 5+, that wound is not lost.

Metalica
Relentless March
  • Can make a normal move and shoot Heavy weapons taking a -1 to their hit rolls.
  • +1” to Normal Movement.

Lucius
The Solar Blessing
  • Treat enemy AP -1 attacks as AP 0 for saving throws.
  • Improve AP by -1 with Heavy Weapons.

Agripinaa
Staunch Defenders
  • Successfully hit on Overwatch with a roll of 5 or 6.
  • Improve AP by 1 when targeting operatives within 4”.

Stygies VIII
Shroud Protocols
  • Enemy ranged attacks beyond 4” receive a -1 to hit.
  • Can Charge while having a Conceal order.

Ryza
Red in Cog and Claw
  • Re-roll wound rolls of 1 for attacks.
  • +1” to Charge.
